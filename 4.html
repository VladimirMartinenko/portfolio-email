3-step maple syrup flapjacks

Ingredients - Makes 16

200g unsalted butter, softened

100g pure maple syrup

150g light soft brown sugar

325g porridge oats

Method

Cooking time: 25 minutes
Prep time: 5 minutes

Preheat the oven to 180°C/gas mark 4. Line a 16 x 25cm baking tin with baking parchment.

Melt the butter in a pan over a medium heat. Stir in the maple syrup and sugar, and simmer until it’s mostly dissolved. Remove from the heat and stir in the porridge oats.

Spoon into the tin and bake for 25 minutes. Remove from the oven and score into 16 pieces using a knife. Leave to cool in the tin before serving.
